Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to contact ECH Property Management?

You can reach us by email at manager@echmanagement.services or by phone at 430-207-8466. We aim to respond within one business day.

When can I view a property that I'm interested in?

Showings are scheduled by appointment. Please apply through our online portal or contact us to coordinate a time that works for you.

How does the application process work?

Apply through our secure online portal. We screen for credit, income, rental history, and background. Most decisions come within 2-3 business days.

What documentation do I need to apply?

Government-issued photo ID, proof of income (recent pay stubs or offer letter), and any prior rental references. Self-employed applicants may submit recent tax returns.

What is the fee for someone to apply for a property?

Application fees are listed on the application page in our portal and are non-refundable. Fees cover the cost of background and credit screening.

Do you require Rental Insurance?

Yes — renter's insurance is required for all our properties. It protects your personal belongings and provides liability coverage.

Do you allow pets?

Pet policies vary by property. Some properties accept pets with an additional pet deposit and monthly pet rent. Service animals are always accepted in accordance with fair housing law.

What are the leasing terms?

Standard lease terms are 12 months. Other terms may be available depending on the property — please ask when applying.

How much is the security deposit?

Security deposits typically equal one month's rent, due at lease signing.

When is my rent due?

Rent is due on the 1st of each month. A grace period extends through the 5th. Late fees apply after the 5th per your lease.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept payments via our online portal (RentRedi) — credit/debit card, ACH bank transfer. Pay online here.

How do I submit a maintenance request?

Submit maintenance requests through your tenant portal at RentRedi, or email us at manager@echmanagement.services. For after-hours emergencies, call 430-207-8466.
