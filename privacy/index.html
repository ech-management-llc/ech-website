Privacy Policy

ECH Property Management (a trade name of E.C.H. Tejas Company LLC) — "ECH," "we," "us." Effective date: July 14, 2026.

This Privacy Policy explains how ECH collects, uses, and protects information, including information related to our text-messaging (SMS) program.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly — such as your name, mobile phone number, email address, and property or work details — and basic technical information when you use our website or applications.

How we use information

We use information to operate our property-management business, communicate with our team, contractors, and customers, provide requested services, and send operational text messages to individuals who have opted in (see below).

Text messaging (SMS) program

ECH operates an internal, two-way operational text-messaging program for registered team members and contractors who have opted in. By opting in, you agree to receive operational text messages from ECH (schedules, showings, work orders, reminders, confirmations, and replies to your requests).

  • Message frequency varies based on your activity and is sent infrequently.
  • Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier and plan.
  • Reply HELP for help or STOP to opt out at any time. After you send STOP, you will receive one confirmation message and no further messages.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile opt-in data and consent are never shared with any third parties. Information may be shared only with service providers that support the messaging program (for example, our messaging platform), solely to deliver the service.

How we share information

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information for third-party marketing. We may share information with service providers who help us operate, and as required by law.

Data security & retention

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information, and retain it only as long as needed for the purposes described here or as required by law.

Your choices

You may opt out of texts at any time by replying STOP. You may contact us to request access to or deletion of your information, subject to applicable law.
